## Detailed Instructions

### 1. Opening the Assessment
- Locate the `supplier-cybersecurity-assessment.html` file
- Double-click to open in your default web browser
- Works offline - no internet required

### 2. Company Information (Required)
Complete these fields at the top:
- Company Name
- Contact Person
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Assessment Date (auto-filled)

### 3. Answer Security Questions

Select one maturity level for each of 39 questions:

| Answer | Points | When to Select |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Not Implemented** | 0 | You don't have this control |
| **Partially Implemented** | 1 | Control exists but inconsistently applied |
| **Implemented** | 3 | Control in place and working consistently |
| **Fully Mature** | 5 | Control optimized with continuous improvement |
| **Not Applicable** | N/A | Question doesn't apply to your business |

**Tip:** Answer honestly - this helps your client understand your security posture and provide appropriate support.

### 4. Track Your Progress
- Watch the progress bar fill as you complete questions
- Your maturity score displays in real-time
- See how many questions you've answered

### 5. Save Your Work

**When you're finished:**
1. Scroll to the bottom of the page
2. Click **"ðŸ’¾ Save Assessment to Disk"**
3. File downloads to your computer as: `security_assessment-yourcompany-date.html`

**The file contains:**
- All your company information
- All your answers
- Your maturity score
- Complete assessment ready to send

### 6. Review and Edit (Optional)

**Need to make changes?**
1. Find the saved file in your Downloads folder
2. Double-click to reopen it
3. All your answers are preserved
4. Make any changes
5. Click "Save Assessment to Disk" again
6. New file includes the updated date

### 7. Submit to Your Client
- Email the final saved HTML file
- Client can open it in any browser
- They'll see your complete assessment and score
